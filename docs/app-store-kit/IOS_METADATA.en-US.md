## Description (Ready to paste)
Echo is a voice input app designed for fast, high-frequency text entry.

Speak naturally, stop recording, and Echo converts your speech to text and inserts it into your current text field.

Great for:
- Messaging
- Email drafting
- Meeting notes
- Mixed Chinese and English input

Key features:
- Transcription after each recording session
- Automatic insertion at the current cursor position
- Local history and playback
- Local storage for traceability
- Optional AI text polishing

Permissions:
- Microphone: used to capture speech for transcription
- (If keyboard extension is enabled) Full Access: used for syncing app/extension settings and results

We continuously improve transcription accuracy, insertion reliability, and overall typing flow.
